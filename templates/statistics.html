OPIOD STATISTICAL ANALYSIS

Visualization of opioid prescription rates

BRIEF SUMMARY

Other aspects of preventable diseases are misuse, addiction, and the overdose of drugs and in this particular case, opioids. One of our pilot studies is starting off examining opioid prescription data. From 2014-2017, opioid use has remained consistent across all states. Future diseases will be showcased in the near future. Additionally, the team at Health Matters is dedicated to linking together the environmental, genetic, and demographic factors that come to play in not just opioid addiction but in other diseases as well.
